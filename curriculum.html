RTNDF Four Year Radio Curriculum for High School Students

Co-written by Alyne Ellis and Carol Lange with a grant from the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation. It is a four year curriculum with daily lesson plans and work sheets to teach radio and journalism to high school students. It is currently in use in schools around the country.
