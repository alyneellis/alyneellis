Alyne Ellis

National Radio Host, Producer, Writer, Editor, Storyteller, Documentarian, Voice-Over Artist, Teacher, and Curriculum Writer

Prime Time Focus has been one of the most popular radio news and features series in America, heard daily on more than 500 radio stations. Features include reports on sports greats, authors, movie stars, policy makers, and everything from travel tips to caregiving and retirement resources. Alyne hosted, wrote, and produced Prime Time Focus from 2007 until AARP's closing of its radio and TV broadcast department in the summer of 2015.
